═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  HOW TO UPDATE THE APP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Every time you get an updated golf-crm.jsx file from Claude,
follow these steps:

STEP 1 — Open the new file in Antigravity (text editor)

STEP 2 — Find these two lines near the very top:
    const SUPABASE_URL = "YOUR_PROJECT_URL_HERE";
    const SUPABASE_KEY = "YOUR_PUBLISHABLE_KEY_HERE";

STEP 3 — Replace the placeholder text with your actual
    Supabase credentials (keep the quotation marks)

STEP 4 — Press Command + S to save the file

STEP 5 — Go to Finder → press Command+Shift+G → type:
    /Users/sethbehrman/gg-crm/src/
    Drop the new golf-crm.jsx in there, replacing the old one

STEP 6 — Open Terminal and run:
    cd /Users/sethbehrman/gg-crm
    vercel --prod

STEP 7 — Wait about 30 seconds. You'll see:
    ✅ Production: https://gg-crm.vercel.app
    That means it's live!
