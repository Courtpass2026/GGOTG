HOW TO OPEN THE APP:
--------------------
This CRM runs as a React app inside Claude.ai.

1. Go to claude.ai in your browser
2. Open your "Great Golf on the Go" Project
3. Upload golf-crm.jsx and ask Claude to "display this artifact"

HOW TO SAVE YOUR DATA (IMPORTANT):
------------------------------------
Right now the app holds data in memory — if you close the browser,
your added clients/expenses/invoices reset to the sample data.

To save your real data permanently, come back to Claude and say:
  "I'm ready to set up the database"
Claude will walk you through connecting Supabase (free) so all
your data saves permanently — step by step, plain English.

CURRENT FEATURES:
-----------------
DASHBOARD
  - Revenue collected, net profit, upcoming events, birthday reminders
  - Outstanding invoice alert with one-tap link to due invoices

CLIENTS
  - Full client profiles: name, email, phone, address, birthdate,
    referral source, notes
  - Search across all clients
  - Birthday countdown badges
  - Outstanding balance badges

EVENTS (Bookings)
  - Track all events: date, location, guests, amount, status
  - Event reminder text shortcut on each booking

FINANCES
  - Overview: revenue, expenses, net profit, category breakdown chart
  - Invoices: filter by All / Due / Paid, one-tap status toggle,
    payment reminder text button on outstanding invoices
  - Expenses: log expenses with category, receipt flag, notes,
    delete option; 11 preset categories

MESSAGES
  - Individual text: 6 templated texts, personalized or generic,
    opens iPhone Messages app
  - Individual email: AI-written drafts, opens Mail app
  - Group email campaigns: 6 campaign types, AI-written,
    personalized (one-at-a-time) or generic BCC blast,
    select individual clients or all at once
